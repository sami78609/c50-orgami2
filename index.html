ORGAMI CAMEL

*Facts On Camel*

    Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

    Mother camels carry their calves up to 14 months before giving birth.

    Some calves are born completely white and turn brown as their adult coat comes in.


ORGAMI PIGEON

*Facts On Pigeon*

    Pigeons have fought alongside humans in World War I and II

    Pigeons are highly sociable animals often seen in flocks of 20-30 birds

    Young dependant pigeons are known as ‘squabs’


ORGAMI PANDA

*Facts On Panda*

    Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day

    Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo

    An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!


ORGAMI CICADA

*Facts On Cicada*

    Cicadas are like tiny violins

    Cicadas have been used in folk medicines

    Cicadas do not bite or sting
